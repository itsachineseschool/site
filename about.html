About Us

Meeting Times & Class Schedule

Classes meet every Sunday, with classes meeting from 9:00 AM until 4:25. There are 5 minute breaks in between each class. The full class schedule is listed below:

  • 王双双古代故事/神话故事: 9:00 AM - 9:45 AM & 9:50 AM - 10:35 AM
  • 王双双成语/地理: 10:50 AM - 11:35 AM & 11:40 AM - 12:25 PM
  • 王双双中文一册: 1:00 PM - 1:45 PM & 1:50 PM - 2:35 PM
  • 王双双中文五册: 2:50 PM - 3:35 PM & 3:40 PM - 4:25 PM

A complete calendar of all Pan Mei CTC events can be found on the calendar page. You can receive SMS alerts from Pan Mei CTC by signing up here.

Location

All classes are held at Berkeley College in Woodland Park, located at 44 Rifle Camp Road, Woodland Park, NJ. You can get directions by using the map.
