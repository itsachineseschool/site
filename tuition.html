Tuition

Tuition prices starts at $480.00 to register for one semester, and there is a 10% discount ($864.00) if registering for two semesters/a full year.

In addition to the tuition, each class has a mandatory textbook fee. The fee varies depending on each class and the duration in which the child takes the class, whether it be one semester or a full year. The prices for each class are listed below:

Class One semester Full year
王双双古代故事/神话故事 $20.00 $40.00
王双双成语/地理 $20.00 $40.00
王双双中文一册 $26.00
王双双中文五册 $26.00

Payments for the 2017-18 year must be received by August 20th, 2017. If payment is received after that date, the payment is subject to a $30.00 late fee.

Payment Methods

Your child's tuition and textbook fee can be paid in one of two methods:

Method 1: Pay using PayPal

Method 2: Pay by check

To pay through check, please make the check payable to Pan Mei CTC and mail it to 33 Pawnee Ave. Oakland, NJ, 07436.
