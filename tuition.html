Tuition

Tuition

Price starts at $480.00 a semester. However, if you pay for an entire year (two semesters), the price is $864.00, which is a 10% discount. There is also a sibling discount. If you register more than one child, there is a 5% discount on the entire purchase. There is no registration or administration fee, but there is a late fee of $30.00 if the payment is received after August 27th, 2017.

Book Fees

There is a mandatory book fee for every class, which must be paid along with the tuition. Specific Fees for each class can be found below.
王双双古代故事/神话故事: $20 book fee for one child for one semester, $40 book fee for one child for one year.
王双双成语/地理: $20 book fee for one child for one semester, $40 book fee for one child for one year.
王双双中文一册: $26 book fee for one child, for both one semester and one year students.
王双双中文五册: $26 book fee for one child, for both one semester and one year students.
